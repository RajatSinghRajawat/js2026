JavaScript Math Methods and Their Uses

Math.round()

Use: Decimal number ko nearest integer me convert karta hai

Math.floor()

Use: Hamesha number ko neeche (down) round karta hai

Math.ceil()

Use: Hamesha number ko upar (up) round karta hai

Math.trunc()

Use: Decimal part hata deta hai, sirf integer part deta hai

Math.abs()

Use: Negative number ko positive banata hai

Math.pow()

Use: Power calculate karta hai (base^exponent)

Math.sqrt()

Use: Square root nikalta hai

Math.max()

Use: Multiple numbers me sabse badi value find karta hai

Math.min()

Use: Multiple numbers me sabse chhoti value find karta hai

Math.random()

Use: Random number generate karta hai (0 se 1 ke beech)
